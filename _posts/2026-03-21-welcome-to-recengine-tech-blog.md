# Building in Public, as a Team

Welcome to the RecEngine Tech Blog.

This site is our shared space for writing about the engineering work behind
recommendation-driven products. We wanted something simple, searchable, and easy
to maintain so the team could publish technical notes without a heavy platform
or editorial process getting in the way.

## What You Can Expect Here

- Architecture writeups for ranking, retrieval, and serving systems
- Post-launch reflections on experiments, performance work, and incidents
- Practical lessons from tooling, observability, and developer experience
- Team notes that turn one-off knowledge into reusable reference material

## Why We Started It

Good engineering decisions deserve a longer shelf life than a chat thread.

As the RecEngine codebase and platform grow, we want a durable home for the
context behind our choices: what we tried, what failed, what shipped, and what
we would do differently next time.

## What Comes Next

- Deep dives into recommendation infrastructure
- Notes on experimentation and evaluation workflows
- Performance and reliability stories from production systems
- Technical walkthroughs of the tools the team builds for itself

If you are part of the team, this blog is here to help you publish what you
learn. If you are reading from outside the team, we hope the details are useful
for your own systems and decision-making.

Thanks for stopping by. More posts are on the way.
